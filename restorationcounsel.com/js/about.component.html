About

Alyssa Meyers is a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of Texas.

Before entering into a private practice setting, Alyssa gained counseling experience in community agencies, as well as several years experience at a crisis-oriented inpatient care facility.

Alyssa is passionate about creating a space of trust and honesty for all clients seeking change in their lives. Specialty areas include: anxiety/panic disorders, depression, past abuse, PTSD, mood disorders, and spirituality.

When Alyssa is not counseling, she loves spending time with family and continuing her musical endeavors by writing and performing music with her band and local church.
