Crisis Resources

Although Restoration Counseling is equipped to handle crisis situations for current clients in session; Restoration Counseling is not a crisis-oriented facility. If you or a loved one are experiencing a crisis or emergency, it is best advised that you call 911, visit your nearest emergency room, or contact one of the following resources:

  • Tri-County Psychiatric Emergency Treatment Center: 800-659-6994
  • National Suicide Prevention Line: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Cypress Creek Hospital: 281-586-7600
  • Kingwood Pines Hospital: 281-404-1001
  • IntraCare North Hospital: 281-893-7200
  • MHMR/Crisis Helpline: 713-970-7000
  • Memorial Hermann Crisis Clinic (Spring Branch): 713-338-MHCC(6422)
  • Teen Crisis Hotline: Text “START” to 741741
  • Gulf Coast Center Crisis Hotline: 866-729-3848
